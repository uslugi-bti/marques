Marques De Guadalmina — a place you’ll want to call home

If you've been searching for a place where both you and your children will truly feel at ease, welcome to Marques De Guadalmina.

This residential community wasn’t designed as just another development project. It was created as a space for real life — for peace, for comfort, for children's laughter, for slow family breakfasts on the terrace and long evenings overlooking the sea and the mountains. Here, you're not rushing through life — you're simply enjoying it.

There’s a well-regarded school, Atalaya Colegio Internacional, just a short walk away — ideal for families with kids. And just around the corner, you’ll find a premium Mercadona supermarket, stocked with everything from fresh produce to prepared meals — perfect for busy family life.

This isn’t a noisy or crowded area. It’s quiet, peaceful, full of clean air and greenery — a hidden corner of calm.

The homes themselves are thoughtfully designed, with spacious layouts and wide terraces where kids can play, and you can relax, sunbathe, enjoy dinner outdoors or simply breathe in the beauty of the view.

The community has the feeling of a botanical garden — palm trees, flowers, birds, even parrots flying overhead. It’s green, serene, and the air is fresh and clean.

You’ll also find a private lake, multiple swimming pools for both adults and children, and a large spa area that invites you to unwind every day — not just on weekends.

Love golf? The courses are right nearby. Craving the sea? The beach is just a few minutes away. Want to shop or dine in Puerto Banús? You can get there in about 15 minutes, comfortably and calmly.

Most importantly, this is a place for people who value comfort, nature, and peace of mind. It’s not a holiday rental zone, not a tourist hotspot — it’s a place to truly live, and live well.

Facts & Figures — Marques De Guadalmina

Property Types and Units

Marques De Guadalmina is a gated residential complex consisting of 123 apartments, developed in multiple construction phases. The complex includes:

  • 1-, 2-, and 3-bedroom apartments
  • Penthouses on the top floors
  • Two-level duplex apartments
  • There are no villas or townhouses — all properties are located in modern multi-storey buildings with elevators, underground parking, and landscaped grounds.

Apartment Sizes

  • 1-bedroom — approximately 60 m²
  • 2-bedroom — from 97 to 119 m²
  • 3-bedroom — from 134 to 182 m²

All sizes refer to built (constructed) area.

Purchase Prices

As of 2025, apartment prices vary depending on size, layout, floor level, views, and finishes:

  • Starting from: €300,000 for smaller units
  • Up to: €700,000 for larger duplexes or penthouses
  • Average price per square meter: €3,800 to €4,200

Rental Prices

Rental prices in Marques De Guadalmina typically range from €2,500 to €5,000 per month. Rates depend on:

  • Number of bedrooms
  • Apartment size and layout
  • Quality of renovation and furnishings
  • Availability of terraces or views
  • Duration and conditions of the lease

Technical Specifications of the Residential Complex

  • Foundation and Structure

    Foundation: Reinforced concrete slab with bored piles.
    Structure: Reinforced concrete columns and solid slab floors.

  • Façades and Roofing

    Façades: Smooth exterior render with painted finish.
    Roofing: Flat walkable roofs with slope formed using cellular concrete, thermal insulation, double-layer asphalt waterproofing, protective mortar layer, and porcelain stoneware tiling (Loft Antracita SPO by Grupo Porcelanosa).

  • Masonry and Partitions

    External walls: Double-brick cavity wall with thermal insulation, air chamber, and interior dry-lining system with 15 mm plasterboard.
    Dividing walls between homes: Double plasterboard layer, glass wool insulation, galvanized steel sheet (0.6 mm), additional glass wool layer, and double plasterboard finish.
    Internal partitions: Drywall system with rock wool insulation.

  • Insulation and Soundproofing

    Floor slabs: Acoustic insulation between units using Impactodan or similar product.
    Walls and roofs: Projected polyurethane foam or extruded polystyrene in walkable terraces.

  • Garage and Communal Areas

    Garage door: Automated vehicle access with magnetic key and remote control.
    Electric car charging: Pre-installed power points in garages.
    Fire safety: Fire prevention systems in compliance with regulations.
    Garage floor: Smoothed concrete finish.
    Elevator: One high-end elevator per building, descending to the garage level, with automatic doors and 6-person capacity.
    TV reception: Satellite dish installed.

  • Flooring

    Interior floors (excluding wet areas, terraces, and patios): Porcelain stoneware Ferroker Aluminio by Grupo Porcelanosa.
    Terraces and patios: Porcelain tile Loft Antracita SPO by Grupo Porcelanosa.

  • Interior Finishes and Fittings

    Kitchens: Walls tiled with rectified ceramic tile Marmi China (Grupo Porcelanosa). Fully fitted with upper and lower cabinets and premium-brand appliances (Siemens, Bosch or AEG): refrigerator, washing machine, dishwasher, vitroceramic hob, extractor hood, oven, and microwave.
    Bathrooms: Main bathroom: Wall tiles Rodano Caliza. Secondary bathrooms: Wall tiles Dover Caliza.
    Ceilings: Plasterboard ceiling on metal framework throughout the house. Removable aluminum ceiling in bathrooms (for access to A/C units).

  • Interior and Exterior Joinery

    Entrance door: Armored door with anti-leverage hinges and 3-point high-security lock.
    Interior doors: Lacquered flat panels with stainless steel handles; kitchen, living room, and hallway doors include glass inserts.
    Wardrobes: Lacquered flat panel doors with interior fittings and hanging rail.
    Windows: Anodized aluminum with thermal break system and micro-ventilation. Double-glazed thermo-acoustic glass: GUARDIAN SUN (4 + chamber + 5) and GUARDIAN SUN (4 + chamber + laminated 5+5) on ground floor. Motorized aluminum shutters with injected thermal insulation.

  • Sanitaryware

    Toilets: Vitrified porcelain by Grupo Porcelanosa.
    Main bathrooms: Arquitect model.
    Secondary bathrooms: Urban C model.
    Bathtubs: Ecolite model.
    Shower trays: Arquitect model, reinforced with fiberglass, anti-slip base.
    Shower columns: Hydromassage Lan Inox (Grupo Porcelanosa).
    Washbasins: Enka model, integrated in Silestone countertops.

  • Taps and Mixers

    Main bathroom: Chrome single-lever NK-LOGIC taps.
    Other bathrooms: ACRO-N taps (All by Grupo Porcelanosa).

  • Hot Water Supply

    Domestic hot water: Individual electric water heaters.
    Communal: Accumulator tanks with pressure pumps for constant flow.

  • Solar Energy

    Solar thermal system for hot water, supporting the individual water heaters. Compliant with Spanish Technical Building Code (CTE).

  • Electrical System

    Mid-to-high grade installation in accordance with Low Voltage Regulation and Common Telecommunications Infrastructure regulations. High-quality electrical switches and sockets.

  • Telecommunications and Media

    TV, telephone, and satellite connection points in the living room, kitchen, and all bedrooms. Video intercom system installed.

  • Security and Home Automation

    Home automation system: alarm control, motion detectors, door sensors, A/C activation.

  • Climate Control

    Individual split-type air conditioning system for both heating and cooling.

  • Painting and Wall Finish

    Walls in main living areas: smooth, colored plastic paint.
    Ceilings: smooth white finish.
    Inside wardrobes: smooth white plastic paint.
